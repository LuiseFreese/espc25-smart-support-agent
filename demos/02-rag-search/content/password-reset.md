# Password Reset

## How to Reset Your Password

If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it for security reasons, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the login page at https://portal.example.com/login
2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link (it may take up to 5 minutes)
5. Click the link in the email (valid for 24 hours)
6. Enter your new password (must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click "Reset Password"

## Password Requirements

- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
- Must be changed every 90 days

## Troubleshooting

**Not receiving reset email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you're using the correct email address registered with your account
- Wait 5 minutes and try again
- Contact support if still not received after 15 minutes

**Reset link expired?**
- Request a new password reset
- Links are valid for 24 hours only

**Password doesn't meet requirements?**
- Ensure your password has all required character types
- Avoid common words or patterns
- Try using a password manager to generate a strong password
