# Invoice Payment and Billing Updates Guide

## Updating Payment Methods

If you need to update your payment method for an invoice, follow these steps:

### For Credit Card Updates

1. **Access the Billing Portal**
   - Go to https://billing.example.com
   - Sign in with your company credentials
   - Navigate to "Payment Methods"

2. **Update Card Information**
   - Click "Add New Payment Method"
   - Enter your new card details:
     - Card number
     - Expiration date
     - CVV code
     - Billing address
   - Click "Save and Set as Default"

3. **Apply to Pending Invoices**
   - Go to "Invoices" tab
   - Find your invoice (e.g., INV-2025-11-045)
   - Click "Change Payment Method"
   - Select your newly added card
   - Click "Update"

### Secure Payment Link

If you prefer not to enter payment details directly:
- Contact support at billing@example.com
- Include your invoice number
- We'll send you a secure payment link within 24 hours
- The link expires after 7 days for security

### Common Scenarios

**Card Expiration Date Changed:**
Simply update the expiration date and CVV in the billing portal. No need to re-enter the full card number.

**Corporate Card Renewal:**
When your corporate card is renewed with a new expiration date and CVV:
1. Update the card details in the billing portal
2. Verify the card ending matches (e.g., ending in 4532)
3. Confirm the update was successful

**Payment Method for Specific Invoice:**
To update payment for a specific invoice (e.g., Invoice #INV-2025-11-045):
1. Navigate to the invoice in the billing portal
2. Click "Change Payment Method"
3. Select or add new payment method
4. Confirm the amount matches (e.g., $2,499.00)
