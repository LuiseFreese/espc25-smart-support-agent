# Billing and Payments

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (for enterprise customers)
- Purchase orders (annual contracts only)

## How to Update Payment Information

1. Log into your account at https://portal.example.com
2. Navigate to **Account Settings** > **Billing**
3. Click **Payment Methods**
4. Select **Add New Payment Method** or edit existing
5. Enter payment details
6. Click **Save**

## Refund Policy

**Standard Refunds**
- Requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- Original payment method will be credited

**Pro-rated Refunds**
- Available for annual subscriptions cancelled mid-term
- Calculated based on unused months
- $50 cancellation fee applies

**No Refunds**
- Services used beyond 30 days
- Custom development work
- Third-party add-ons

## Billing Cycles

- **Monthly**: Billed on the same day each month
- **Annual**: Billed once per year with 15% discount
- **Usage-based**: Billed at end of month based on consumption

## Invoice Questions

To request an invoice:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Invoices**
2. Select the billing period
3. Click **Download PDF** or **Email Invoice**

For corrections or disputes:
- Submit ticket with invoice number
- Include explanation of issue
- Allow 3-5 business days for review

## Failed Payments

If your payment fails:

1. Update payment information immediately
2. Service may be suspended after 7 days
3. Reactivation fee ($25) applies if suspended
4. Data deleted after 30 days of non-payment
