# Duplicate Charges and Double Billing Resolution Guide

## What to Do If You Were Charged Twice

If you notice duplicate charges or were charged twice on your bill, follow these steps to resolve the issue quickly:

### Immediate Actions

1. **Verify the Duplicate Charge**
   - Log into your account at https://billing.example.com
   - Go to **Billing** > **Transaction History**
   - Check if both charges show the same:
     - Transaction ID
     - Amount
     - Date and time
     - Description

2. **Check for Common Causes**
   - **Browser refresh**: Did you click "Pay" multiple times?
   - **Temporary holds**: Some banks show pending charges that may disappear
   - **Subscription renewal + manual payment**: Check if one is automated
   - **Family account**: Multiple users on same payment method

### Resolution Steps

**For Confirmed Duplicate Charges:**

1. **Contact Support Immediately**
   - Email: billing@example.com
   - Phone: 1-800-SUPPORT (1-800-787-7678)
   - Include:
     - Both transaction IDs
     - Screenshots of duplicate charges
     - Your account number

2. **Automatic Refund Process**
   - Duplicates detected by our system are refunded automatically within 24-48 hours
   - Check your email for refund confirmation
   - Refund appears on your statement within 5-7 business days

3. **Manual Refund Request**
   - If no automatic refund within 48 hours, submit a ticket
   - Go to **Support** > **Submit Request**
   - Select category: "Billing Issue - Duplicate Charge"
   - Attach proof: bank statement or transaction screenshot

### What Happens Next

- **Within 1 business day**: Support team reviews your case
- **Within 2 business days**: Refund is processed
- **Within 5-7 business days**: Refund appears on your bank statement
- **Email confirmation**: You'll receive confirmation at each step

### Preventing Future Duplicate Charges

1. **Wait for confirmation page** before closing browser
2. **Check "Processing" message** - don't click Pay again
3. **Use auto-pay** to avoid manual payment errors
4. **Enable email notifications** for all transactions

### Special Cases

**Charged Twice for Subscription Renewal:**
- One charge may be the current month
- Another may be a pro-rated adjustment
- Check invoice details for breakdown

**International Charges Showing Twice:**
- Currency conversion can create separate line items
- Check if amounts are identical or different
- One may be the actual charge, one the authorization hold

**Multiple Cards Charged:**
- Check if you have multiple payment methods on file
- Review if a backup payment method was triggered
- Update default payment method preferences

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
A: 5-7 business days after processing (2 business days to process + 3-5 days bank processing)

**Q: Will I be charged a fee?**
A: No fees for duplicate charges caused by system errors or processing issues.

**Q: Can I get a refund in cash?**
A: Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

**Q: What if I was charged twice on different cards?**
A: Both charges will be refunded to their respective cards.

**Q: My bank says it's a pending charge, not duplicate?**
A: Wait 3-5 business days for pending charges to clear. If both post, then request refund.
