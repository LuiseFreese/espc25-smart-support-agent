# Refund Policy

## Overview
Contoso IT Services offers refunds within 30 days of purchase for all services and subscriptions.

## Eligibility
- Service must be cancelled within 30 days of initial purchase
- No refunds for services used beyond the trial period
- Annual subscriptions: Pro-rated refunds available within first 30 days

## How to Request a Refund

1. **Email Support**
   - Send request to: support@contoso.com
   - Subject line: "Refund Request - Order #[YOUR_ORDER_NUMBER]"

2. **Include Required Information**
   - Order number or invoice ID
   - Purchase date
   - Reason for refund request
   - Preferred refund method (original payment method or credit)

3. **Processing Time**
   - Refund requests reviewed within 2 business days
   - Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days
   - Refund appears on original payment method within 10 business days

## Special Cases

### Cancelled Services
- If service was cancelled mid-billing cycle, partial refund may apply
- Contact support for pro-rated calculation

### Technical Issues
- If refund is due to technical problems, support may offer:
  - Extended trial period
  - Service credit
  - Full refund plus 1 month free

### Subscription Renewals
- Auto-renewals can be refunded if cancelled within 7 days of renewal date
